Our Story

Where innovation meets tradition in every cup

Susanto Liman

Founder & CEO

"My journey with coffee began in the mountains of Indonesia, where I first discovered the magic of coffee cultivation. After years of learning and perfecting the art of coffee making, I founded MR.COFFEE with a vision to bring innovation to the traditional coffee experience. Today, we continue to push boundaries while staying true to our roots, creating a space where technology and tradition coexist harmoniously."

Our Mission

At MR.COFFEE, our mission is to make every day special through the perfect cup of coffee. We believe in:

  • Innovation in every aspect of coffee making
  • Sustainability in our sourcing and operations
  • Creating memorable experiences for our customers
  • Building a community of coffee enthusiasts

Our Vision

We envision a future where MR.COFFEE becomes a global symbol of innovation in the coffee industry, while maintaining our commitment to quality and customer experience. Our goal is to:

  • Expand our presence while maintaining our unique identity
  • Lead the industry in sustainable practices
  • Create innovative coffee experiences
  • Build a global community of coffee lovers
